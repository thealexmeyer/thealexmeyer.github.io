Intro

Hello! My name is Alex, and I'm a journalist who likes writing about the environment and Appalachia. I'm currently finishing up my senior year at Ohio University, where I study journalism. This is a portfolio website I made using my (somewhat limited) knowledge of HTML and CSS. I've uploaded story links and PDFs that you can check out if you so desire.

A brief summary of my experience: I've covered and edited news for OU's student-run media outlet, The Post, and wrote feature stories for The Columbus Dispatch. As a junior, I wrote longform, enterprise pieces about southeast Ohio on topics like water quality, fracking and the impacts of coal mining. In summer 2017, I wrote about energy and lake vegetation in Western New York for The Chautauquan Daily. I'm also currently producing a longform and multimedia project on coal in Appalachia for my senior thesis.

Beyond writing, I enjoy reading about history, making videos, and biking outdoors. Feel free to reach out via the contact info I've listed below.
