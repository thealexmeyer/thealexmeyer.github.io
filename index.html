Intro

Hello! My name is Alex, and I'm a journalism student at Ohio University. I'm from Columbus, Ohio and love writing about the environment and Appalachia. This is my attempt at a portfolio website that I made with my (somewhat limited) knowledge of HTML and CSS. I've uploaded story links and my resume that you can check out if you so desire.

A brief summary of my experience: I've covered and edited news for OU's student-run media outlet, The Post, and wrote feature stories for The Columbus Dispatch. As a junior, I wrote longform stories about southeast Ohio on topics like water quality, fracking and the impacts of coal mining. In summer 2017, I wrote about energy and lake vegetation in Western New York for The Chautauquan Daily. I'm also currently producing a longform project on coal in Appalachia for my senior thesis.

Beyond writing, I enjoy reading about history, making videos, and long-distance biking. Feel free to reach out via the contact info I've listed below.
